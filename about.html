Jennifer Olsen About Me

Hello There,

I'm a freelance web developer and designer. I moved from Washington, D.C. to Portland, Oregon in 2009 and recently graduated from the PCC Website Development and Design program. After a stint working at the incredible Powell's Books, I decided to work on the greatest library humans have ever built (otherwise known as the World Wide Web). When not playing around with Photoshop or a good text editor, you'll likely find me with my nose firmly buried in a book.

I'm available for projects in all shapes and sizes, but I specialize in starter websites for individuals and businesses. I work primarily in HTML, CSS (SASS whenever possible), and WordPress, but I'm also comfortable with javascript and PHP.

You can find out more about me on LinkedIn (and GitHub if you're nerdy), or take a look at my portfolio (if you haven't already). Or you could just send me a message.
